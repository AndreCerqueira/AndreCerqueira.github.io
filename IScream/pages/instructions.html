I Scream

How to play

The goal of the game is to accumulate as many points as possible before melting away.


The Player

Use the arrow keys to navigate the player around the board.

Game Areas


Cold Area

Enter this area to gain 1 second and earn 3 points.

Warm Area

Step into this area to earn 1 point.

Hot Area

Entering this area will cost you 3 seconds.

Items


Ice cube

Use this to turn all areas into cold areas for the next move.

Icy friend

Use this to gain 5 points instantly. Each additional Icy Friend in your inventory at the time of use grants an extra 5 points.

Rocky

This item occupies a slot in your inventory and cannot be removed during the current game.
